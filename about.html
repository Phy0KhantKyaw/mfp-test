About Us

Myanmar Thiha Group of Companies was established in 2001. Our company is 100% local own business in Myanmar, which with diversified investment specialize in selling and distributing of Rain Flower Fire Extinguisher, Fire Fighting Equipment and Oxygen (Industrial Use and Medical Use), Refilling Fire Extinguisher, Installing Solar Systems & Fire Protection Systems, E&C and Mining services in Myanmar. Our company provides one-stop service for customers and our employees are also well-trained, skillful and qualified to be able to support the customers by solving their problems effectively and giving useful suggestions before and after sales.
